Concentration Tool
Sometimes your negative thoughts can cause problems with staying focused

Has it ever happened to you when you are taking a test that you get to a difficult question,

you don’t know the answer and you start to feel a beginning of panic starting in your stomach

and rising through your body?

And then when you go to the next question you still carry that panic with you?

You can’t stop thinking about the previous question you didn’t know.
How do you do this during the test?

First you must become aware

when your mind is wandering

Then you choose a word to say to yourself

that will bring you back to the task at hand
